facalities at our campus

library
Our school has three well equipped Libraries. The Nursery Library aims at developing the love for books among the junior students. 'Books are our friends' is the motto of this library, which is well equipped with colourful, illustrative and attractive books that include picture dictionaries, story books, educative materials, etc. The school has a well stocked Senior Library which makes our student intellectually strong and sound. The lure and love for books bind the thoughts & dreams of the students, and enables them to build strong edifices for the future. sports
Sports forms an integral part of curriculum at DAV NOIDA, where sports spark to make a mark. Considered thrust is given to the physical development of the students. Various inter - school and inter - house competitions are organized to give full scope to the students to enhance their skills & talents. For imparting training / Practices in indoor games, Indoor Game's Hall, Gym, Yoga Centre and Table Tennis Hall are also available in the school premises. Our school also has playgrounds for outdoor games like Cricket, Basketball, Football, Volleyball,Handball, etc.in the school campus to develop sportsmanship traits, team-sprit and a sense of belongingness among the energetic and enthusiastic players. Each year our school has won prizes/ trophies in the district, state and national level sports tournaments, to keep up the sporting spirit among our students. library
Library facilities are provided for almost all the classes and students are encouraged to read books of their liking for pleasure and information.Access to the Reference Library is available to students of Secondary Section.Audio and Video cassettes are also available to students, teachers and parents.Damage to a book or loss of book is to be made good by the offender.For the student who fails to return the book to the Library even by the end of the academic session, his/her result of the Annual Examination will be withheld unless and until the book, has been returned and the fine paid. Music Class
The Music Room offers sheer splendor, where melodious vocies and chords are created and praticed. The dedicated teachers spot the talents and groom them up. Under focussed guidance of teachers, our students have won many laurels for the school. labs
The school has well equipped and spacious integrated science lab,physics lab,chemistry lab & biology lab providing modern gadgets / apparatus etc required by students to quench their experimentation thirst. Here scientific attitude is developed among the students, along with ability to analyze, collate, compute, integrate and deduce by experimenting the academic knowledge gained in the class / through books. In short, we endeavour to grow future scientists of the world in our labs.
